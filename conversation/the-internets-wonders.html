Vocabulary List

  • Internet: A global network connecting computers and devices to share information.
  • Search Engine: A website that finds information on the Internet based on keywords (e.g., Google).
  • Recipe: A set of instructions for preparing food or drink.
  • YouTube: A website for watching and sharing videos online.
  • Social Media: Online platforms for sharing photos, messages, and updates with friends (e.g., Facebook, Instagram).
  • Video Call: A live, face-to-face conversation over the Internet using camera and microphone (e.g., WhatsApp).
  • Convenient: Easy to use or access, saving time and effort.
  • Secure: Protected against danger, loss, or unauthorized access.
  • Password: A secret word or phrase used to access an account or system.
  • Email: Electronic mail for sending messages and files over the Internet.
  • News: Information about recent events, often read online or watched on TV.
  • Bus Schedule: A timetable showing bus departure and arrival times.
  • App: A software application designed for specific tasks on a mobile device or computer.
  • Online Safety: Practices to protect personal information and stay safe while using the Internet.
  • Website: A collection of web pages accessed through the Internet under a single domain name.
