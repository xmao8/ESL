Adjective Clauses Worksheet: Life Goals & Problems

This worksheet focuses on **Adjective Clauses** (also known as Relative Clauses), which help you combine sentences and add detail. For each pair of sentences below, combine them into one sentence using an appropriate relative pronoun (**who, whom, whose, which, that**) or relative adverb (**when, where, why**). Remember to think about whether the clause is essential or non-essential, and use commas accordingly.
