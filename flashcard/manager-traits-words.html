Flashcards – Personality Adjectives

ambitious
Having a strong desire and determination to succeed.
courteous
Polite, respectful, or considerate in manner.
demanding
Requiring much effort, attention, or skill.
friendly
Kind and pleasant in attitude or behavior.
funny
Causing laughter or amusement; humorous.
hardworking
Putting a lot of effort and diligence into work.
interesting
Holding or catching attention; engaging.
patient
Able to accept delays or problems without frustration.
quiet
Making little or no noise; calm or reserved.
respectful
Showing polite regard or consideration.
serious
Showing deep thought or sincerity; solemn.
strict
Enforcing rules firmly; not allowing freedom.
