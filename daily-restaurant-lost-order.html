In a Restaurant: The Lost Order

Characters:

  • LIAM: A waiter, friendly and a little busy.
  • CHLOE: A customer, polite and a little confused.
  • NOAH: Another customer, a bit impatient.

Setting:

A table in a busy restaurant.

(The scene opens with CHLOE sitting at a table. LIAM approaches.)

LIAM: Hello! Welcome. Are you ready to order?

CHLOE: Yes, thank you. I would like the... um... the chicken salad sandwich, please.

LIAM: Okay, one chicken salad sandwich. Anything to drink?

CHLOE: Just water, please.

LIAM: Great. I'll be right back with your water.

(Liam goes to get the water. NOAH approaches the table.)

NOAH: Excuse me?

CHLOE: Oh, hello. Are you waiting for this table?

NOAH: No, no. I ordered a pizza about twenty minutes ago. Have you seen a waiter?

CHLOE: Our waiter just left. He's wearing a blue shirt.

NOAH: Blue shirt... okay. Thanks.

(Liam returns with Chloe's water.)

LIAM: Here's your water.

CHLOE: Thank you.

NOAH: (To Liam) Excuse me! I ordered a pizza a while ago. Where is it?

LIAM: Oh, I'm so sorry! What kind of pizza did you order?

NOAH: Pepperoni! A large pepperoni pizza.

LIAM: Pepperoni... Hmm. I don't have a pepperoni pizza here. Are you sure you told me?

NOAH: Yes, I'm sure! I've been waiting a long time.

CHLOE: Maybe there was a mistake?

LIAM: (Thinking) Let me check the kitchen again. I apologize for the wait.

(Liam hurries off. Chloe looks at Noah sympathetically.)

CHLOE: This restaurant is very busy tonight.

NOAH: I'm very hungry. I hope they didn't forget my order.

(Liam returns, looking a little flustered.)

LIAM: I am so sorry! There was a mix-up in the kitchen. Your pepperoni pizza will be ready in about five minutes.

NOAH: Five more minutes?

LIAM: Yes, I'm very sorry. Can I get you something while you wait? Maybe some bread?

NOAH: (Sighs) Okay, some bread would be nice.

LIAM: Great. I'll get that for you right away. And your sandwich will be out soon too, ma'am.

CHLOE: Thank you.

(Liam goes to get the bread. Noah looks a little less impatient.)

CHLOE: Hopefully, the food will be good.

(The lights fade.)

Discussion Questions

  • What did Chloe order?
  • Why was Noah impatient?
  • What was the problem with Noah's order?
  • How did Liam try to make up for the mistake?
  • Have you ever had a lost order in a restaurant? What happened?
  • What is the difference between being "impatient" and "flustered"?

Vocabulary List

  • waiter 🔊: a person who serves food and drink at the tables in a restaurant.
    • Example: The waiter took our order quickly.
    • Example: Liam is a friendly waiter.
  • confused 🔊: unable to understand something or think clearly.
    • Example: She looked confused by the difficult question.
    • Example: Chloe was a little confused when Noah approached.
  • impatient 🔊: having or showing a tendency to be quickly irritated or provoked when waiting.
    • Example: Noah became impatient while waiting for his pizza.
    • Example: Don't be so impatient; the bus will be here soon.
  • approach 🔊: to come near or nearer to someone or something.
    • Example: The cat slowly approached the bird.
    • Example: Liam approached the table to take the order.
  • sympathetically 🔊: in a way that shows sympathy.
    • Example: Chloe looked at Noah sympathetically.
    • Example: The teacher listened sympathetically to the student's problem.
  • flustered 🔊: agitated or confused.
    • Example: Liam looked a little flustered when he returned from the kitchen.
    • Example: She gets easily flustered when she has too many tasks.
  • mix-up 🔊: a confusion or mistake.
    • Example: There was a mix-up with the room bookings.
    • Example: Liam said there was a mix-up in the kitchen.
  • apologize 🔊: to express regret for something done or said.
    • Example: He apologized for being late.
    • Example: Liam apologized for the wait.
  • look forward to 🔊: to feel pleasure because you are going to do something or something is going to happen.
    • Example: I'm really looking forward to my vacation.
    • Example: Noah was looking forward to his pizza.
