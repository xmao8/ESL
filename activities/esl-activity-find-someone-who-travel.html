Find Someone Who: Travel in America

Walk around and ask your classmates questions to find someone who fits each description. When you find someone, write their name in the box. Try to find a different person for each box!

Find someone who...

...has visited a U.S. national park (e.g., Yellowstone, Grand Canyon).
...has seen the Atlantic Ocean in the U.S.
...has seen the Pacific Ocean in the U.S.
...has visited a city with a famous landmark (e.g., Statue of Liberty, Golden Gate Bridge).
...has traveled to more than 3 different U.S. states.
...has been to a U.S. state that experiences all four seasons distinctly.
...dreams of visiting Alaska or Hawaii.
...has driven more than 500 miles in one trip across the U.S.
...has visited the U.S. capital in Washington D.C.
