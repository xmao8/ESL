Irregular Verbs

Irregular verbs do not make the past simple (V2) and past participle (V3) with -ed. You must learn their special forms. Use V2 for the past simple and V3 after have/has/had (present/past perfect) and in the passive voice.

I went home at 7. (past simple of go)
She has gone home. (present perfect, V3)
The door was broken by the wind. (passive, V3)

Quick Overview
