About ESLnote

ESL note — Learn English Together

Welcome to ESLnote.com! I created this website to share English teaching and learning materials that teachers and students can use freely. Many of these materials come from my own experience teaching ESL intermediate and intermediate-high classes. Some content was created with the help of AI, and some comes from the public domain. Everyone is welcome to use the resources on this website for free.

Categories

  • Learning Flashcards — Expand your vocabulary with interactive flashcards on everyday topics like food, jobs, and more.
  • Daily Conversations — Practice real-life dialogues to get ready for common speaking situations.
  • Grammar Essentials — Learn English grammar with clear examples and simple explanations.
  • Reading Sections — Read engaging texts and try comprehension exercises to improve your reading skills.
  • Listening Materials — Build your listening skills with audiobooks and videos, including classic literature and movies.
  • Practices — Reinforce your learning with interactive grammar and vocabulary exercises that make practice fun and effective.
