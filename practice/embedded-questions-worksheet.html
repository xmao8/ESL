Embedded Questions Worksheet

Instructions

Practice forming embedded questions. For each direct question below, choose one of the introductory phrases and combine it with the question to form a complete, polite embedded question. Remember to change the word order and punctuation correctly!

Introductory Phrases:

  • Do you know...
  • I was wondering...
  • Can you tell me...
  • I wonder...
  • He asked...
  • I'm not sure...
  • Could you explain...
  • I have no idea...
  • We need to find out...

Now, practice with these questions about a community.
