Life Vocabulary Practice

Instructions: Choose the best word or words from the options provided to fill in the blank(s) in each sentence.

Part 1: Personal Training

1. To become a successful personal trainer, you need a strong understanding of human ___________ and exercise physiology.
(a) biology (b) anatomy (c) chemistry (d) physics
2. Many trainers work with clients to help them achieve their fitness ___________, such as weight loss or muscle gain.
(a) hobbies (b) sports (c) goals (d) interests
3. Creating personalized workout ___________ is a key skill for a personal trainer.
(a) games (b) plans / routines (c) equipment (d) theories
4. Good communication and ___________ skills are essential for building rapport with clients.
(a) technical (b) artistic (c) interpersonal (d) mechanical
5. Some personal trainers specialize in certain ___________ groups, like athletes or seniors.
(a) age (b) friend (c) population (d) hobby
6. Staying ___________ on the latest fitness trends and research is important for professional growth.
(a) secret (b) outdated (c) updated (d) confused
7. A personal trainer might assess a client's current fitness level through various ___________ and measurements.
(a) stories (b) assessments (c) guesses (d) opinions
8. Providing motivation and ___________ is a crucial part of a trainer's role.
(a) payment (b) equipment (c) encouragement (d) criticism

Part 2: Career

9. Choosing a fulfilling ___________ path often involves considering your interests and skills.
(a) travel (b) career (c) hobby (d) sport
10. Continuous ___________ and development can lead to career advancement.
(a) resting (b) learning (c) delaying (d) ignoring
11. Networking with professionals in your field can open up new ___________ and opportunities.
(a) problems (b) doors (c) secrets (d) arguments
12. Setting clear ___________ goals can help you stay focused and motivated in your career.
(a) financial (b) personal (c) career (d) travel
13. Maintaining a healthy ___________-life balance is important for overall well-being.
(a) school (b) social (c) work (d) play
14. Adapting to changes in the job ___________ is crucial for long-term success.
(a) market (b) weather (c) food (d) music
15. Strong ___________-solving skills are valued in almost any profession.
(a) cooking (b) cleaning (c) problem (d) artistic
16. Ethical behavior and ___________ are fundamental to a positive work environment.
(a) dishonesty (b) lateness (c) integrity (d) rudeness

Part 3: Family Life

17. Spending quality ___________ with family strengthens bonds and creates lasting memories.
(a) money (b) time (c) food (d) silence
18. Effective ___________ is key to resolving conflicts and maintaining healthy family relationships.
(a) fighting (b) communication (c) ignoring (d) hiding
19. Supporting each other's ___________ and aspirations is important in a family.
(a) chores (b) secrets (c) dreams (d) complaints
20. Creating family ___________ and traditions can provide a sense of belonging and connection.
(a) rules (b) arguments (c) rituals (d) problems
21. Balancing ___________ responsibilities with work demands can be challenging.
(a) pet (b) car (c) family (d) friend
22. Open and honest ___________ about feelings and needs is vital in a family.
(a) avoidance (b) communication (c) guessing (d) blaming
23. Making time for ___________ activities together can enhance family unity.
(a) separate (b) individual (c) shared (d) secret
24. Showing ___________ and appreciation for family members fosters a positive atmosphere.
(a) disrespect (b) anger (c) love (d) jealousy
