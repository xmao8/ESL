Practice: Present Perfect Progressive (Health)

Complete each sentence with the present perfect progressive form: have/has + been + verb-ing. Use time words like for, since, lately, all week when needed.
Example: I ___ (walk) → I have been walking.

  1. I (try) to drink two liters of water every day this month.
  2. She (sleep) poorly since the baby arrived.
  3. We (cook) at home more instead of ordering fast food lately.
  4. My brother (train) for a 10K to improve his heart health.
  5. How long (you / cough)?
  6. They (avoid) sugary drinks since January.
  7. My grandpa (take) his blood pressure at home every morning this week.
  8. (she / do) her physiotherapy exercises regularly?
  9. The research team (study) stress and sleep patterns for months.
  10. I (see) a therapist to manage my anxiety lately.
  11. The students (practice) mindfulness for ten minutes each day.
  12. He (recover) slowly after the operation.
  13. Our class (learn) about healthy habits since the start of the term.
  14. You (skip) breakfast again lately, haven’t you?
  15. My neighbor (walk) 5,000 steps a day to lower his blood pressure.
