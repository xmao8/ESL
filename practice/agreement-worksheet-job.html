Agreement in English (Jobs) – Multiple Choice

Choose the correct option for each sentence. Focus on agreement (subject–verb, pronouns, and demonstratives).

Reminder:
  • Singular subject → singular verb (often -s in present simple).
  • Plural subject → plural verb (no -s in present simple).
  • this/that + singular noun; these/those + plural noun.
  • Pronouns should match the person/thing: his / her / their, it / they.
1
My manager ____ emails every morning.
2
The managers ____ emails every morning.
3
Each employee ____ a password for the company system.
4
Everyone in the office ____ on time today.
5
There ____ a meeting at 9:00.
6
There ____ two meetings this afternoon.
7
The list of job applicants ____ on my desk.
8
The team ____ late on Fridays. team = one group
9
The staff ____ helpful when customers have questions.
10
This report ____ important for the interview.
11
____ documents are confidential. Please don’t share them.
12
That job ____ interesting, but the hours are long.
13
Those jobs ____ interesting, but the hours are long.
14
Maria is a nurse. She loves ____ work.
15
The technicians finished ____ tasks before lunch.
16
Every worker should wear ____ ID badge at work.
17
A supervisor helps new employees when ____ need support.
18
All of the equipment ____ ready for the job site.
19
All of the workers ____ ready for the job site.
20
My coworkers and I ____ responsible for customer service.

Teacher tip: After checking, ask students to explain why each answer is correct.
