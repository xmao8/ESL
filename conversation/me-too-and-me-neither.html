Travel Preferences: Me too or Me Neither

Scene: Lucy and Marcus are looking at travel brochures in a waiting room.

LUCY: Oh, wow, look at this picture of the Grand Canyon! It's absolutely stunning. I really want to go there someday.

MARCUS: Me too! I've always dreamed of seeing it in person. The scale of it just looks incredible.

LUCY: I also love the idea of exploring big cities. Paris, Tokyo, New York... I can't decide which one to visit next.

MARCUS: Me neither. They all have so much to offer! But honestly, I'm not a huge fan of crowded tourist spots.

LUCY: Me neither, actually. Sometimes the crowds really take away from the experience. I prefer finding the quieter, local spots.

MARCUS: Exactly! And I don't really enjoy long flights. They just exhaust me.

LUCY: Me neither. Eight hours in a tiny seat? No, thank you! I much prefer road trips.

MARCUS: Me too! There's something great about the freedom of the open road. You can stop whenever you want.

LUCY: Totally. And I'm not a very good planner, so I don't usually book everything in advance.

MARCUS: Me neither. I like to be spontaneous and see where the trip takes me. It makes for better stories!

LUCY: Definitely! I haven't done much international travel lately, though.

MARCUS: Me neither. I've mostly been exploring different parts of our own country. There's so much to see here!

LUCY: Me too! I just got back from a trip to Florida, and it was amazing.

MARCUS: Oh, really? I've never been. What was your favorite part?
