Vocabulary List

Here are some important words and phrases from the reading. Try to guess the meaning, then check the definitions.

  • Boulevard
    A wide city street, usually with trees on both sides.
    "We walked along the beautiful boulevard in Paris."
  • Brioche
    A soft, slightly sweet bread from France, often eaten for breakfast.
    "She ordered coffee and brioche at the cafÃ©."
  • In bloom
    Having flowers that are open and at their best.

    The supply of items (such as flowers) that a seller has to sell.
    "The shop's stock was fresh and colorful."

    A famous university in Paris, especially known for arts and humanities.
