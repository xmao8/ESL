Job and Professional Flashcards

🧹 Service and Maintenance Jobs

Cleans buildings like schools, offices, and hospitals.
Janitor / Cleaner
Cleans and maintains homes or hotel rooms.
Housekeeper
Takes care of outdoor spaces like lawns, gardens, and parks.
Landscaper / Groundskeeper

🍔 Food Service Jobs

Prepares food in restaurants or cafeterias.
Cook / Line Cook
Washes dishes, pots, and pans in kitchens.
Dishwasher
Takes food orders and serves customers in restaurants.
Server / Waiter / Waitress
Prepares and serves food in fast food restaurants.
Fast Food Worker

🛒 Retail and Customer Service Jobs

Handles payments at a store or restaurant.
Cashier
Helps customers find and buy items in a store.
Sales Associate
Assists customers with questions or problems, often by phone or in person.
Customer Service Representative

🧰 Skilled and Labor Jobs

Builds houses, roads, and buildings.
Construction Worker
Paints buildings, walls, and other surfaces.
Painter
Fixes cars, trucks, or machines.
Mechanic

🏥 Healthcare Jobs

Helps elderly or sick people with daily tasks at home.
Home Health Aide
Assists nurses in caring for patients in hospitals or care homes.
Nursing Assistant (CNA)
Supports doctors by taking vital signs, preparing exam rooms, and helping with paperwork.
Medical Assistant

🏫 Education and Childcare Jobs

Assists teachers in the classroom, especially in early education.
Teacher’s Aide
Cares for children in daycares or after-school programs.
Childcare Worker

🚛 Transportation and Warehouse Jobs

Delivers packages, food, or other items to homes and businesses.
Delivery Driver
Packs, loads, and moves goods in storage or shipping facilities.
Warehouse Worker

🎓 Professional Jobs – Education and Academia

Teaches and conducts research at a college or university.
Professor
Leads and manages a school.
Principal
Helps students with academic, personal, or social challenges.
School Counselor
Manages books and information resources in libraries.
Librarian

⚖️ Professional Jobs – Law and Government

Makes legal decisions in a court of law.
Judge
Represents clients in legal matters.
Lawyer / Attorney
Enforces laws and keeps communities safe.
Police Officer
Supports individuals and families through social services.
Social Worker

🏥 Professional Jobs – Healthcare and Medicine

Diagnoses and treats illnesses.
Doctor / Physician
Performs operations on patients.
Surgeon
Treats teeth and oral health problems.
Dentist
Prepares and gives medicine to patients.
Pharmacist
Treats mental and emotional issues.
Psychologist
Helps people cope with life challenges and mental health.
Therapist / Counselor

💼 Professional Jobs – Business and Finance

Manages money and financial records for businesses or individuals.
Accountant
Studies market trends to help businesses or clients invest wisely.
Financial Analyst
Hires and supports employees in a company.
Human Resources Manager

🖥️ Professional Jobs – Science and Technology

Designs and builds machines, structures, or systems.
Engineer
Creates computer programs and apps.
Software Developer
Researches and studies natural phenomena to gain knowledge or solve problems.
Scientist
