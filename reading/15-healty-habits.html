15 Healthy Habits

15 Healthy Habits for Everyday Life

These habits are simple actions you can do every day to improve your health. Read the list and think about which habits you already do and which ones you want to start.

  1. Drink enough water every day: Keep a water bottle with you and sip often.
  2. Sleep 7‚Äì8 hours each night: Go to bed and wake up at the same time.
  3. Eat fruits and vegetables daily: Aim for several colors on your plate.
  4. Exercise regularly (3+ times a week): Walk, run, dance, or do yoga.
  5. Brush your teeth twice a day: Protects your teeth and gums.
  6. Wash your hands before eating: Prevents the spread of germs.
  7. Take short breaks from work or study: Rest your eyes and mind.
  8. Limit sugary snacks and drinks: Choose whole foods and water instead.
  9. Practice good posture when sitting: Keep your back straight and shoulders relaxed.
  10. Spend time outdoors in fresh air: Sunlight and nature can boost your mood.
  11. Meditate or practice mindfulness: Focus on your breathing for a few minutes.
  12. Eat a balanced breakfast every morning: Start your day with energy.
  13. Avoid smoking and limit alcohol: Protects your lungs and liver.
  14. Stay connected with friends and family: Healthy relationships support mental health.
  15. Manage stress with hobbies or relaxation: Try music, reading, or gentle stretching.

Comprehension Questions

  1. Which three habits do you already practice every day? Give examples.
  2. Which habit is the most difficult for you? Why?
  3. How can good posture help your health?
  4. Why should you limit sugary snacks and drinks?
  5. What are two ways to manage stress mentioned in the list?
  6. Choose one habit and explain how you will add it to your routine this week.
