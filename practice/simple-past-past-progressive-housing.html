Review: Use the simple past for completed actions in the past (e.g., *She **moved** last year*). Use the past progressive (was/were + verb-ing) for an action in progress at a specific time, or as background when a shorter action interrupts it (e.g., *He **was painting** the wall when the phone **rang***).
