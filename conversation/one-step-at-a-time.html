One Step at a Time

Characters:

  • Lina: in her 30s, an ESL student who dreams of opening a bakery
  • Marco: a middle-aged man, wants to become a nurse
  • Sara: a retired teacher learning English for travel and volunteering

Scene: A community English class after school. The students are cleaning up and talking.

Lina: (Sighs) I really needed this class today. Sometimes I feel like my goals are too far away.

Sara: What do you mean? You always come prepared. You’re doing great.

Lina: Thanks, Sara. But... my dream is to open a bakery. I love baking, and I even worked in one back home. But here, I don’t know where to start.

Marco: That’s a beautiful dream, Lina. You bake amazing cookies! But yeah... starting a business takes time.

Sara: Maybe you could take a small business class? Or sell your baked goods at local markets first?

Lina: That’s a good idea. But I also need better English to talk to customers. That’s why I come here.

Marco: Me too. My goal is to become a nurse. I’ve always wanted to help people. I used to be a hospital assistant, but I need a certification here.

Sara: Wow! That’s amazing. Nursing is a noble job.

Marco: It’s hard, though. I work full-time, and then I study at night. Sometimes I feel too tired to keep going.

Lina: But you haven’t given up. That’s the important part.

Sara: You know, I’m retired now. I taught school for 30 years. Now my goal is to travel and volunteer. Maybe teach children in other countries.

Marco: That sounds so meaningful! But... you’re still learning English?

Sara: (Laughs) Yes! I want to feel confident when I travel. And I believe we should never stop learning.

Lina: That’s inspiring. I guess our goals are all different, but we share the same path—learning and growing.

Marco: Exactly. One step at a time.

Sara: Let’s help each other. Lina, I’ll help you practice customer phrases. Marco, I can help with medical vocabulary.

Lina: And I’ll bring cookies next week!

Marco: Deal!

Sara: Deal!

[End Scene]

Vocabulary List

  • Sighs 🔊 /saɪz/ (v.): makes a long, deep, audible exhalation, expressing sadness, relief, tiredness, etc.
  • Goals 🔊 /ɡoʊlz/ (n.): the object of a person's ambition or effort; an aim or desired result.
  • Prepared 🔊 /prɪˈpɛrd/ (adj.): made ready for use or consideration.
  • Dream 🔊 /driːm/ (n.): a cherished aspiration, ambition, or ideal.
  • Bakery 🔊 /ˈbeɪkəri/ (n.): a place where bread, cakes, etc., are made or sold.
  • Business 🔊 /ˈbɪznɪs/ (n.): a person's regular occupation, profession, or trade.
  • Customers 🔊 /ˈkʌstəmərz/ (n.): a person or organization that buys goods or services from a store or business.
  • Nurse 🔊 /nɜːrs/ (n.): a person trained to care for the sick or infirm, especially in a hospital.
  • Certification 🔊 /ˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ (n.): the action or process of providing someone with an official document attesting to a status or level of achievement.
  • Noble 🔊 /ˈnoʊbl/ (adj.): having or showing fine personal qualities or high moral principles.
  • Retired 🔊 /rɪˈtaɪərd/ (adj.): having stopped working because of age.
  • Volunteer 🔊 /ˌvɒlənˈtɪr/ (v.): freely offer to do something.
  • Confident 🔊 /ˈkɒnfɪdənt/ (adj.): feeling or showing certainty about something.
  • Inspiring 🔊 /ɪnˈspaɪərɪŋ/ (adj.): having the effect of inspiring someone.
  • Meaningful 🔊 /ˈmiːnɪŋfʊl/ (adj.): having a serious, important, or useful quality or purpose.
