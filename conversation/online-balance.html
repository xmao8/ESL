Vocabulary List

  • Excessive: more than necessary, too much, or extreme in degree.
  • Distracted: unable to concentrate because one‚Äôs attention is diverted by something else.

  • Usage: the way in which something is used; the act of using something.
  • Limit: a boundary or maximum amount that should not be exceeded.
  • Offline: not connected to or using the Internet or a network.
  • Enrich: to improve or enhance the quality or value of something.
  • Reminders: prompts or signals to help one remember to do something.
  • Schedule: a plan that lists times and events; to arrange for something to happen at a certain time.
  • Disciplined: showing self-control and obedience to rules or a plan.
  • Hobbies: activities done regularly for pleasure during one's free time.
  • Family time: periods when family members spend time together to strengthen relationships.
