Subject-Verb Agreement Worksheet: Life Goals

This worksheet focuses on Subject-Verb Agreement, a key element for accurate English. Read each sentence carefully and fill in the blank with the correct verb form that agrees with its subject. Pay close attention to singular and plural subjects, as well as negative sentence structures. This exercise will help you master agreement rules, especially with more complex sentence structures related to personal aspirations and achievements.
