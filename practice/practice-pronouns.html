Pronoun Practice: Fill-in-the-Blank

Instructions: Choose the correct pronoun from the options in parentheses to complete each sentence.

Name: _________________________
Date: _________________________
1. This laptop is (my / mine), so please don't touch it.
2. (He / Him) works as a senior software engineer at the new startup.
3. Did (you / your) receive the urgent email about the project deadline?
4. The students prepared for the exam by (themselves / theirselves).
5. Could you please hand me (this / these) folder on the desk?
6. The new policies apply to (we / us) as well as the management team.
7. The final decision is now entirely (their / theirs).
8. I taught (me / myself) how to manage my time more effectively.
9. (This / These) are the reports I need for the meeting tomorrow.
10. The manager thanked (they / them) for their hard work on the challenging task.
11. The big office on the top floor is (our / ours).
12. (She / Her) will lead the next team meeting.
13. Please make (you / yourselves) comfortable while you wait.
14. Is this pen (your / yours)? I found it on the floor.
15. (That / Those) are the documents that need to be signed.
16. I believe (I / me) can complete this assignment by the end of the day.
17. They need to submit (their / theirs) proposal by Friday.
18. Look at (that / those) large building in the distance; it's impressive!
19. My colleague and (I / me) are working on a joint project.
20. The company's new policy has proved beneficial to (itself / itself).
