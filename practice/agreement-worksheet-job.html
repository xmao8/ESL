Agreement in English (Jobs) – Fill-in-the-Blank

Fill in each blank with the correct word(s). Focus on agreement: subject–verb agreement, pronoun agreement, and demonstratives.

Tips:
  • Singular subject → singular verb (often -s in present simple).
  • Plural subject → plural verb (no -s in present simple).
  • this/that + singular noun; these/those + plural noun.
  • Pronouns should match the person/thing they replace: his/her/their, it/they, etc.
1
My manager emails every morning.
2
The managers emails every morning.
3
Each employee a password for the company system.
4
Everyone in the office on time today.
5
There a meeting at 9:00.
6
There two meetings this afternoon.
7
The list of job applicants on my desk.
8
The team late on Fridays. (team = one group)
9
The staff helpful when customers have questions.
10
This report important for the interview.
11
documents are confidential. Please don’t share them.
12

13
Those jobs interesting, but the hours are long.
14
Maria is a nurse. She loves work.
15

16
Every worker should wear ID badge at work.
17
A supervisor helps new employees when need support.
18
All of the equipment ready for the job site.
19
All of the workers ready for the job site.
20
My coworkers and I responsible for customer service.
