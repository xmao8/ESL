Past Tense vs. Present Perfect Tense - Grocery Shopping

Practice using the **past tense** and the **present perfect tense** correctly. Remember: The past tense is for a finished action at a specific past time. The present perfect is for an action that happened at an unspecified past time, or a past action that has a result in the present.
