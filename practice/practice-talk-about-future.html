Practice: Future Forms Worksheet

Instructions: Read each sentence carefully and choose the most appropriate future form from the options given, or fill in the blank with the correct form. Be prepared to explain your choice based on the summary provided.

Part 1: Choose the Best Future Form

  1. "I'm really hungry!" "Don't worry, I ______ you a sandwich right now."

    Answer: b) will make

  2. Look at those dark clouds! It ______ rain very soon.

    Answer: b) is going to

  3. My shift at work ______ at 9:00 AM tomorrow.

    Answer: c) starts

  4. We ______ dinner with my parents tonight. We've had it planned for a week.

    Answer: a) are having

  5. I promise I ______ forget to pick up the groceries.

    Answer: b) won't

  6. "The doorbell is ringing!" "I ______ get it."

    Answer: b) will

  7. I think it ______ be sunny all weekend.

    Answer: b) will

  8. They ______ to the gym after work. They've already packed their bags.

    Answer: a) are going

  9. My train to the city ______ at 7:15 PM.

    Answer: c) leaves

  10. "I need help with this math problem." "No problem, I ______ help you after dinner."

    Answer: b) will

  11. She ______ a new job next month. She just signed the contract.

    Answer: a) is starting

  12. I'm so tired. I think I ______ go to bed early tonight.

    Answer: b) will
