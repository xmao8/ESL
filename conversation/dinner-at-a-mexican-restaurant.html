Dinner at a Mexican Restaurant

Characters:

  • LIAM: A customer, in his 20s, excited but unfamiliar with Mexican food.
  • MARIA: His friend, a local, helping him order.
  • WAITER: A friendly, helpful server.

Setting: A vibrant, colorful Mexican restaurant.

(The scene opens with Liam and Maria seated at a table. A basket of tortilla chips and a bowl of salsa are on the table. Liam is looking at the menu, which has a lot of Spanish words.)

LIAM: Wow, this menu is huge! What's the difference between a **burrito**, a **taco**, and an **enchilada**? They all sound like they have similar ingredients.

MARIA: That’s a good question. A taco is a small, soft or crispy tortilla folded around fillings. A burrito is a large flour tortilla wrapped around a bigger mix of fillings, and an enchilada is a corn tortilla rolled around a filling and covered in a chili sauce and cheese before being baked.

LIAM: Okay, I think I get it. So, a burrito is like a big wrap?

MARIA: Exactly. And they are all delicious! Let’s start with some **guacamole**. It's a dip made from avocados, and it's amazing with the chips.

LIAM: Guacamole sounds good. What about drinks? Is that a margarita I see on the menu?

MARIA: Yes, a **margarita** is a very popular cocktail here. It’s made with tequila, lime juice, and a little bit of salt on the rim of the glass. You should try one!

(A waiter approaches their table with a pad to take their order.)

WAITER: Hola! Are you ready to order, or do you need a little more time?

MARIA: We're ready, thank you! We'll start with an order of guacamole. For me, I’ll have the **carne asada** tacos, please. And a regular margarita.

WAITER: And for you, sir?

(Liam looks at the menu again, then at Maria for reassurance.)

LIAM: I think I'll try the enchiladas. What kind of **filling** can I get?

WAITER: We have chicken, beef, cheese, or vegetables. And you can choose between a red or green sauce. Our green sauce, or **salsa verde**, is a little bit spicy.

LIAM: I'll have the chicken enchiladas with the red sauce. And just a soda, please.

WAITER: Excellent choices! I'll get those started for you.

(The waiter walks away. Liam smiles, feeling much more at ease.)

LIAM: Thank you, Maria! I would have been completely lost without you.

MARIA: No problem! I'm happy to introduce you to my favorite food. You're going to love it.

(The two friends cheers with their water glasses, excited for their food to arrive. FADE OUT.)

Vocabulary List

  • Burrito 🔊 /bʊˈriːtoʊ/ (n.): A dish consisting of a large flour tortilla wrapped around a filling of meat, beans, and cheese. Example: He ordered a beef and bean **burrito**.
  • Taco 🔊 /ˈtɑːkoʊ/ (n.): A dish consisting of a small, soft or crispy tortilla folded around fillings such as meat, vegetables, and cheese. Example: I love to get **tacos** on Tuesdays.
  • Enchilada 🔊 /ˌɛntʃɪˈlɑːdə/ (n.): A dish consisting of a corn tortilla rolled around a filling, covered with a chili pepper sauce, and baked. Example: The cheese **enchilada** was covered in a rich red sauce.
  • Guacamole 🔊 /ˌɡwɑːkəˈmoʊli/ (n.): A dip made from mashed avocados, often with onions, tomatoes, and lime juice. Example: The chips came with a side of delicious **guacamole**.
  • Margarita 🔊 /ˌmɑːrɡəˈriːtə/ (n.): A cocktail made with tequila, lime juice, and an orange-flavored liqueur, often served with salt on the rim of the glass. Example: We ordered a frozen strawberry **margarita**.
  • Carne asada 🔊 /ˈkɑːrneɪ əˈsɑːdə/ (n., Spanish): Marinated and grilled steak, often served in tacos or burritos. Example: The **carne asada** was grilled to perfection.
  • Filling 🔊 /ˈfɪlɪŋ/ (n.): The food put inside a taco, burrito, or enchilada. Example: What kind of **filling** would you like for your taco?
  • Salsa verde 🔊 /ˈsælsə ˈvɛrdeɪ/ (n., Spanish): A green sauce typically made from tomatillos, chili peppers, and cilantro. Example: I prefer the spicy **salsa verde** over the red sauce.
  • Tortilla 🔊 /tɔːrˈtiːjə/ (n.): A thin, flat bread made from corn or flour. Example: We had fresh corn **tortillas** with our dinner.
  • Salsa 🔊 /ˈsælsə/ (n.): A spicy sauce made from chopped tomatoes, onions, and chili peppers. Example: The restaurant made its own fresh **salsa**.
